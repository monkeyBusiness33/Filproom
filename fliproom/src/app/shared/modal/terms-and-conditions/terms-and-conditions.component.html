Commission Rates

Sale prices within the below brackets will be subject to the specified commission rates. These are automatically applied to show your final payout price after an item sales.

  • £0-£320 per item is 26%
  • £321-£999 per item is 22%
  • £1000-10000 per item is 17%

When Pricing ALWAYS set your prices at .99 for example £159.99

The ownership is down to you to make sure your inventory is up to date and accurate at all times.

As soon as you no longer have an item available for sale, it must be removed from your inventory on the platform.

If your payout expectation changes, this must be adjusted on the platform.

When uploading an item it will show you the cheapest price we have live at the moment. (With this in mind price accordingly, as the website will list & sell the lowest priced first)

When we sell your item, you will be notified via email, you then must accept this within 12hrs.

Any orders not accepted within this time will be rejected and the sale will be lost.

Any rejected orders will be subject to a 7% penalty charge to cover transactional fees.

We cover all shipping & create shipment labels for you.

Once you have accepted a sale we will send a label to you directly via email or WhatsApp.

You must then use the tracking number from the shipment label and input this onto the platform to confirm shipment has been made.

As soon as tracking has been input to the order your payout will be released.

All shipments must be dropped off within 24 hours of receiving shipment label.

By agreeing to these terms you are subject to follow them. Failure to do so, could result in being removed from the platform.
